Lovely Sweets

Lovely Bake Studio at Lovely Professional University (LPU) is part of LPU's School of Hospitality and is specifically designed to give students hands-on experience in the baking and pastry arts. This studio is a place where students can learn, practice, and experiment with various baking techniques, from traditional cakes and pastries to more advanced baking innovations.
