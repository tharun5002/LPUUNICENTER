Food Outlets at Lovely Professional University

Domino's Pizza

Domino's Pizza at Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a popular fast-food outlet offering a wide range of pizza options. Known for its convenience and affordability, Domino's caters to the diverse tastes of students, faculty, and staff, making it an ideal choice for quick meals or late-night cravings.

Exclusive Chocolate Outlet

This specialty store on the LPU campus is perfect for chocolate lovers, offering a wide range of premium, high-quality chocolates and chocolate-based products. Whether for yourself or as a gift, this outlet is a sweet treat haven.

Symposium: Where Taste Meets the Myth

Symposium: Where Taste Meets the Myth blends culinary arts with cultural storytelling. It brings food enthusiasts, culture lovers, and academics together to explore the connection between food and mythology, creating a one-of-a-kind event at LPU.

House of Candy Roll Express

Known for its unique candy rolls and sweet treats, this outlet offers a fusion of traditional and contemporary candy-based desserts, perfect for satisfying any sweet tooth.

Chicago Delights

Offering a variety of classic American fast food, including burgers, sandwiches, fries, and snacks, Chicago Delights is a favorite spot for hearty, satisfying meals on campus.
