Explore LPU's Popular Spots

Le Broc Grocery Store

Le Broc Grocery Store is a local store near Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Phagwara, Punjab. It's a go-to spot for students looking to grab daily essentials, snacks, and more.

Café Coffee Day (CCD)

Café Coffee Day (CCD) is a popular hangout spot at LPU, known for its wide range of beverages and snacks. Students flock to CCD to unwind, study, or chat with friends in a cozy atmosphere.

Canadian Pizza

Canadian Pizza at LPU offers a range of delicious pizzas, perfect for a casual meal between classes or for gathering with friends. Enjoy a tasty treat in a relaxed setting.
